Property Description

2100 El Camino Real offers a unique opportunity to occupy a Class A office building in the heart of Silicon Valley. Just one block south of Stanford University and two blocks from the California Avenue shopping district, College Terrace Centre is located in one of the most desirable neighborhoods in Silicon Valley. College Terrace Centre is in a central location within the greater Bay Area and has easy access to Highways 101 and 280.

2100 El Camino Real offers an idyllic space for high-end technology and professional service firms. College Terrace Centre offers two floors of Class A office space for a total of approximately 45,472 square feet over an underground garage. Targeting a LEED Platinum certification, this project features operable windows, bicycle storage, premier finishes, photo voltaic panels, multiple outdoor areas, corner offices and private patios. Tenants at College Terrace Centre will also enjoy the amenity of an onsite grocery store with delivery services.
